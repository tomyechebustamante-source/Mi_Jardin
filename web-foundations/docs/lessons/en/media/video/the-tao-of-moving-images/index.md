## On Video Optimization

1. _"The longest video loads instantly when never played. The shortest video crushes the user when poorly encoded."_

2. _"Master Chen encoded a 500MB video to 5MB. His student asked: 'Did you lose quality?' Chen replied: 'I lost only what the eye could not perceive and the network could not deliver.'"_

3. _"The wise developer serves ten video formats. The enlightened developer serves only what the browser requests. The master serves AV1 to the future and H.264 to the present."_

4. _"Compress not for the screen you see, but for the connection you cannot measure."_

5. _"A 4K video on a 3G connection is not ambitionâ€”it is cruelty against the user's patience."_

## On Autoplay and Control

6. _"Autoplay without sound is respect. Autoplay with sound is assault. The user who does not choose to hear does not consent to hear."_

7. _"The student asked: 'Should I autoplay?' The master showed him a page with three autoplaying videos. The student's speakers screamed. The master said: 'You have your answer.'"_

8. _"The video that plays without permission is like a guest who enters without knocking. Even if welcome, the intrusion is remembered."_

9. _"Give the user a pause button. Give them a mute button. Give them control. This is the three-fold path to respect."_

## On Captions and Accessibility

10. _"A video without captions is a locked door to the deaf user. You would not build a website with only locked doors."_

11. _"The caption that says '[music playing]' is better than no caption. The caption that says 'video' is worse than silence."_

12. _"Write captions for your grandmother who cannot hear. Write them for the search engine that cannot hear. Write them for yourself when the audio fails."_

13. _"Captions are not optionalâ€”they are essential. The video that speaks only to the hearing speaks to half the world."_

## On Performance and Loading

14. _"Load what is seen. Defer what is hidden. The page that loads all videos loads no video fast."_

15. _"The student said: 'My videos look beautiful!' The master checked the Network tab and said: 'Your users have left before seeing them.'"_

16. _"Preload metadata, not megabytes. The user who waits for metadata is patient. The user who waits for megabytes is gone."_

17. _"Lazy loading is not lazinessâ€”it is wisdom. Load the video when the user is ready, not when the page is ready."_

## On Responsive Video

18. _"Serve a 1920px video to a 375px phone, and the gods of bandwidth will curse your house for seven generations."_

19. _"One video for all screens is like one story for all ears. It fits no one perfectly and everyone suffers."_

20. _"The master writes: `<video><source media="...">`. The novice writes: `<video src="huge-video.mp4">`. The user pays the price."_

21. _"Aspect ratio is not decorationâ€”it is respect. Respect for the mobile user's screen. Respect for the desktop user's viewport."_

## On Video Formats

22. _"MP4 for compatibility, WebM for openness, AV1 for the future. Know the nature of each, and choose with wisdom."_

23. _"The animated GIF masquerading as video is the drunk uncle at the wedding: sometimes entertaining, often embarrassing, always taking up too much space."_

24. _"H.264 is the past. AV1 is the future. VP9 is the bridge. The master serves all three, and the browser chooses wisely."_

## On Background Video

25. _"The background video that obscures the text is like a painting that blocks the doorâ€”beautiful, but fundamentally misunderstanding its purpose."_

26. _"Background video must be silent. Background video must be short. Background video must be optional. This is the three-fold law."_

27. _"The hero video that autoplays is not a hero. It is a 10MB obstacle between the user and their goal."_

28. _"Give the user a pause button for background video. Some users find motion uncomfortable. Respect their preference."_

## On Third-Party Embeds

29. _"The YouTube embed that loads 1MB of JavaScript is not freeâ€”it costs the user's time and data."_

30. _"Self-host when privacy matters. Use platforms when convenience matters. Know the difference, and choose with wisdom."_

31. _"The facade pattern is not deceptionâ€”it is respect. Show the thumbnail, load the player on demand. This is the way."_

## On Accessibility & Ethics

32. _"The deaf user cannot hear your video, but they can read it through captions. Write clearly. Write truthfully. Do not say 'video123.mp4'."_

33. _"The video without a pause button is not engagementâ€”it is imprisonment. The user must always have control."_

34. _"Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Some users find motion uncomfortable. Your video is not worth their discomfort."_
