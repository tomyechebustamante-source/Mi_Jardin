## On Image Optimization

1. _"The heaviest image weighs nothing when never loaded. The lightest image crushes the user when poorly placed."_

2. _"Master Li compressed a 5MB image to 50KB. His student asked: 'Did you lose quality?' Li replied: 'I lost only what the eye could not see and the network could not bear.'"_

3. _"The wise developer serves ten image formats. The enlightened developer serves only what the browser requests. The master serves WebP to Chrome and JPEG to the past."_

4. _"Optimize not for the device you hold, but for the connection you cannot see."_

5. _"A 4K image on a 320px screen is not luxuryâ€”it is violence against the user's data plan."_

## On Lazy Loading

6. _"Load what is seen. Defer what is hidden. The page that loads everything loads nothing fast."_

7. _"The student asked: 'Should I lazy load?' The master showed him a page that loaded 100 images at once. The student's browser wept. The master said: 'You have your answer.'"_

8. _"The image below the fold does not exist until the user scrolls. Why then do you make it exist at page load?"_

9. _"Eager loading is greed. Lazy loading is wisdom. But loading nothing is enlightenmentâ€”question whether the image serves a purpose."_

## On Alt Text

10. _"An image without alt text is a locked door to the blind user. You would not build a website with only locked doors."_

11. _"The alt text that says 'image' is worse than no alt text. It is a sign that points to itself."_

12. _"Write alt text for your grandmother who cannot see. Write it for the search engine that cannot see. Write it for yourself when the image fails to load."_

13. _"Decorative images speak in silence: alt=''. Content images must sing their purpose aloud."_

## On Responsive Images

14. _"Serve a 2000px image to a 375px phone, and the gods of bandwidth will curse your house for seven generations."_

15. _"The srcset is not complexityâ€”it is respect. Respect for the mobile user's data. Respect for the desktop user's screen. Respect for the device you have not yet imagined."_

16. _"One image for all screens is like one shoe size for all feet. It fits no one perfectly and everyone suffers."_

17. _"The master writes: `<img srcset='...' sizes='...' />`. The novice writes: `<img src='huge-image.jpg' />`. The user pays the price."_

## On Image Formats

18. _"PNG for transparency, JPEG for photos, SVG for logos, WebP for the future. Know the nature of each, and choose with wisdom."_

19. _"The animated GIF is the drunk uncle at the wedding: sometimes entertaining, often embarrassing, always taking up too much space."_

20. _"SVG scales infinitely, yet weighs less than a single JPEG pixel. This is the paradox of vector over raster."_

## On Performance

21. _"The fastest image to load is the image not loaded. Question whether the hero needs to be a hero image."_

22. _"Preload the critical. Defer the decorative. Remove the unnecessary. This is the three-fold path to enlightenment."_

23. _"The student said: 'My images look beautiful!' The master checked the Network tab and said: 'Your users have left before seeing them.'"_

24. _"A 3-second load time is not slow because of technologyâ€”it is slow because of your images. The server is blameless; the developer is not."_

## On Design Philosophy

25. _"Use images to enhance the message, not to be the message. Words remain when images fail."_

26. _"The background image that obscures the text is like a painting that blocks the doorâ€”beautiful, but fundamentally misunderstanding its purpose."_

27. _"Fifteen stock photos of diverse people pointing at laptops do not make your website diverse. They make it a clichÃ©."_

28. _"The logo need not be large. The sun is distant yet illuminates all."_

## On Accessibility & Ethics

29. _"The visually impaired user cannot see your images, but they can hear them through alt text. Speak clearly. Speak truthfully. Do not say 'image123.jpg'."_

30. _"Autoplay video is not engagementâ€”it is assault. The user who does not choose to hear does not consent to hear."_
